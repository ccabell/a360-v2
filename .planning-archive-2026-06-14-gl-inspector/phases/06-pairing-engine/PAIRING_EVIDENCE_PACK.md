## Common Pairs (32)

### Non-Botox Neurotoxin + HA Filler Pairs (20 pairs: #6-25)

**Pairs:** Daxxify/Dysport/Jeuveau/Xeomin each paired with Vollure/Voluma/Lyft/RHA Redensity/SKINVIVE

**Tier:** common | **Confidence:** HIGH (category-level evidence)

**Corpus evidence (shared across all 20):**
- All neurotoxins share the same BoNT-A mechanism as Botox. The 90-woman study and expert consensus for neurotoxin + filler applies at the category level.
- Product-specific evidence is less abundant for non-Botox toxins, hence common rather than canonical.
- Dr. David Eccleston confirms nuanced differences between neurotoxins ("subtle nuances") but the complementarity with fillers is mechanism-based, not brand-specific.

**Differentiators:**
- Daxxify: Longer duration (peptide formulation) may reduce treatment frequency in combination plans
- Dysport: Broader diffusion pattern, faster onset; well-documented in multiple podcast episodes
- Jeuveau: Often positioned on price; newer market entrant
- Xeomin: Pure neurotoxin (no complexing proteins); potentially lower antibody formation risk in long-term combination plans
