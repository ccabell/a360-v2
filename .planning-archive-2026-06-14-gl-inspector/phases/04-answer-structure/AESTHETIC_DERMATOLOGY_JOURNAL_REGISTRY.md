# Aesthetic Medicine & Dermatology Journal Registry for Global Library Phase 04 (Source Ingestion)

This is a curated list of high-authority journals in aesthetic medicine, dermatology, cosmetic procedures, and related fields — organized by specialization. Use this registry for Phase 04 (source ingestion) discovery, Phase 05 (concern language mining), and ongoing monitoring of new evidence.

**Notation:**
- **TIER 1**: Highest authority, peer-reviewed, physician-facing, strong aesthetic focus
- **TIER 2**: Strong specialty journals, peer-reviewed, mixed clinical/aesthetic scope
- **TIER 3**: Open access or hybrid, specialized topics, rapid publication
- **OA** = Open Access (full or partial), **Hybrid** = subscription + OA options

## TIER 1 — FLAGSHIP AESTHETIC & COSMETIC JOURNALS

### Injectables & Product-Focused
1. **Journal of Cosmetic Dermatology**
   - Publisher: Wiley
   - Focus: Cosmetic dermatology, injectables, fillers, botulinum, lasers, skin quality
   - Frequency: Monthly
   - Official journal of International Academy of Cosmetic Dermatology (IACD) & Canadian Association of Aesthetic Medicine (CAAM)
   - Coverage: Comprehensive — botulinum, fillers, peels, lasers, dermabrasion, liposuction, hair removal, skin care, photodamage, psychological issues, medicolegal
   - Authority rank: 9/10
   - URL: https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/journal/14732165

2. **Journal of Drugs in Dermatology (JDD)**
   - Publisher: SanovaWorks
   - Focus: Drugs, treatments, procedures in dermatology
   - Frequency: Monthly
   - Includes aesthetic, clinical dermatology, injectables, devices
   - Authority rank: 8/10
   - URL: https://www.jddonline.com/

3. **Journal of Clinical and Aesthetic Dermatology (JCAD)**
   - Publisher: Matrix Medical Communications
   - Focus: Clinical & aesthetic dermatology; practical, evidence-based
   - Frequency: Monthly
   - Peer-reviewed; open-access on PubMed Central
   - Authority rank: 8/10
   - URL: https://jcadonline.com/

### Surgery & Procedural (Aesthetic Focus)

## MONITORING STRATEGY FOR GL PHASE 04+

**Recommended subscription model:**
1. **Core monthly subscriptions** (6 journals): ASJ, JCAD, JDD, Derm Surg, J Cosm Derm, Plast Recon Surg
2. **Open-access automated feed** (5 journals): Cureus, Derm & Therapy, SKIN, BMC Derm, JCD&T
3. **Quarterly deep-dive reviews** (3 journals): JAAD (highest impact), J Invest Derm (mechanisms), Facial Plast Surg (techniques)
4. **Conference proceedings** (annual): ASAPS, ASDS, AAFPRS supplements

**Update frequency:**
- Monitor core monthly journals for topic alerts + table of contents
- Quarterly bulk-review of accumulated articles
- Annual assessment of emerging journals and new society publications
