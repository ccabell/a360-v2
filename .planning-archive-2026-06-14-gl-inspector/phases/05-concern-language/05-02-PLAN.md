**CRITICAL FILTERS — what to extract vs reject:**

EXTRACT (patient speech about appearance):
- "I look tired all the time" -> Tear Trough Hollowing / Brow Ptosis (or cluster alias)
- "my eleven lines" -> Frown Lines / Glabellar Lines
- "these lines from my nose to my mouth" -> Nasolabial Folds
- "I hate my double chin" -> Submental Fullness
- "my skin just looks dull" -> Skin Quality Improvement
- "people think I'm angry" -> Frown Lines / Glabellar Lines
- "my crow's feet are terrible" -> Crow's Feet

REJECT (do NOT add as aliases):
- Provider education language ("periorbital volume deficit", "rhytides", "collagen remodeling")
- Objections/fears ("I don't want to look frozen", "what about downtime", "is it expensive")
- Treatment names used as concern descriptions ("I need Botox" is not a concern)
- Generic vague statements ("I just want to look better" — too vague to route)
