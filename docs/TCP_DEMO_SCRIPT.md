# TCP Builder — Stage Demo Script

> The Treatment & Care Plan calculator. Route: **`/dashboard/tcp`** (sidebar → **TCP**).
> Clean and instant — the hero patient runs from a cached fixture, prices computed live.

## Before you go on

- Open `/dashboard/tcp`. Confirm the **Orange Twist** header.
- Use the **STAGE-READY** patient: **Danielle Brooks** (only stage-guaranteed patient).

## The walk (≈60 seconds)

1. **Select patient → Danielle Brooks**, click **Continue**.

2. **Care plan.** A clean, itemized plan builds from her consultation:
   - Header line: her goal (*refreshed look before her daughter's October wedding*) + the deadline chip.
   - **Recommended treatments stack into a priced plan** — Vollure and Sculptra come pre-checked, each with a one-line rationale, a quantity stepper, and a per-unit price. Subtotal updates live.
   - **Stack more:** check **Botox**, **SKINVIVE**, or **Ultherapy** and watch the plan total climb in real time. Adjust any quantity or price inline.
   - The summary closes like a real quote: **subtotal → package discount → plan total**, then **pay today / 6 months / 12 months** with a per-month and per-day figure.
   - **Say:** *"This is the plan she leaves with — itemized, priced, and financed."* Click **Next**.

3. **Review.** The client-presentable care-plan document — itemized treatments, total, and financing. **Copy plan** drops it as text; **Finalize plan** locks it in.

## Honesty notes (if asked)

- Recommendations, rationale, and concern/FDA matches are real Global Library data.
- **Pricing is illustrative** (synthetic per-unit prices, no real practice pricing) — labeled on the plan.

## If something goes wrong

- Always demo **Danielle** — she's cached and offline-safe.
- If the plan looks empty, make sure at least one treatment is checked.
