## Flagged for Review

1. **PAH in patient education:** Included a dedicated "Important Safety Note" section describing PAH. This is an ethical disclosure requirement. Reviewers should confirm the tone is honest and clear without creating disproportionate alarm. The framing used: "rare but real," "material risk," "your provider will review it with you."

2. **"10-25% reduction" not mentioned in patient education** — deliberately omitted from patient-facing content because the percentage is a population average and could create false expectations. Only "meaningful, visible reduction" language used. Confirm this is the right choice.
