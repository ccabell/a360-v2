## Flagged Items for Review

1. **Narrow indication scope:** Kybella content emphasizes the FDA-only submental indication strongly — confirm this framing is appropriate (off-label body uses exist in the literature but are not detailed here).

2. **Post-treatment swelling pre-communication:** Described as the most critical patient satisfaction driver — clinical reviewer to confirm the 2-4 week swelling characterization is accurate.

3. **Candidacy: skin laxity as relative contraindication** — removing fat from an area with significant skin laxity may worsen appearance. Confirm this is stated appropriately for the clinical lens.
